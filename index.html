Lamario Bros is a dual-threat dynasty powerhouse — elite at the top with Lamar Jackson anchoring the SF premium, elite youth at receiver with Nabers and Hunter, and a surprisingly deep TE room with Andrews in TEP scoring. The RB corps is volatile but loaded with youth (Achane, Wright, Bigsby, Skattebo). C.J. Stroud is a real QB2 asset at age 24 in a loaded Texans offense — not a streamer, a bounce-back cornerstone. The biggest concern is veteran aging risk at WR (Evans, Meyers) and RB depth behind Achane. The 2027 pick capital from Nxbosta could become the most valuable chips in the league depending on that team's performance.

  • Lamar Jackson — Unanimous dynasty QB1 in SF, arguably the #1 overall asset in the league. Age 28, peak window is now.

Needs & Weaknesses
